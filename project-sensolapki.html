The Brief

SensoŁapki needed an identity that communicates care, safety and joy, centred on sensory activities - instantly readable to a parent searching for a toddler club.

01. Concept

Where the name, the audience and the feeling met.

About the club

SensoŁapki ("senso-paws") is a club for the youngest children, built around sensory activities that support early development — touch, movement, sound and play. The audience is parents of toddlers looking for a safe, nurturing place to spend time together.

The idea

The name joins "senso" (the senses) with "łapki" (little paws/hands) — so the hand became the heart of the mark. A small palm holding a heart signals care and the tactile, hands-on nature of every activity.

Identity goals

  • Feel warm, safe and friendly — never clinical.
  • Stay playful and full of energy for young children.
  • Read clearly at any size, from an app icon to a wall sign.
  • Work in full color and in a single-color version.
