Hi, I'm Roksana!

I'm a graphic designer and UX/UI Designer with a background in programming.

Since I was a child, I've loved drawing and retouching photos in graphic-design software. Later I went to a high school with a graphic-design and IT track, where I learned how to design simple websites and create 3D graphic elements.

During my engineering studies, I discovered my passion for designing websites and mobile apps — I learned what UX/UI Design is and fell in love with this world.

My interest in UX/UI stems from that creative drive and the desire to be part of a team that values research and human-centered design. I want to join a team where I can grow my skills to build digital products that are both beautiful and functional.

I'm enthusiastic about sharpening my current skills and learning new ones to create the best products possible — products that improve processes and deliver amazing experiences for people.

In my free time I sing, paint, and travel — drawing inspiration from culture and nature. Let's connect!
